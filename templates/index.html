Your shapefiles are ready

Each layer is a complete shapefile — .shp .shx .dbf .prj .cpg plus .shp.xml metadata. Points are EPSG:4326, lines EPSG:32645. ArcGIS rebuilds its optional .sbn/.sbx index automatically.

Lines are drawn feeder by feeder from the pole numbering tree — never across feeders.
Runs locally · nothing leaves your computer.
